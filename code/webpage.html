Dome Pools, Magaliesburg, South Africa

Dome Pools is a wilderness hiking venue, involving an easy walk over a hill and along a stream to some of the biggest and deepest rock pools in the Magaliesberg, making it a fantastic venue for swimming in the summer months.

Why Do I like Dome Pools?

  • The views
  • Connecting with nature
  • The Pools
