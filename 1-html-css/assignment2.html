Kruger National park

South africa's most thrilling African safari destination.

The Kruger National Park is South Africa's largest wildlfe sanctuary with nearly 2 million ha (4.9 million acres) of unrivalled wilderness and wildlife land. Boasting an exceptional diversity of the game, not only is it a home to the Big Five, but it is home to more different species of wildlife than any other natural sanctuary in Africa.

The Big Five

Why visit the Kruger National park?

  1. The Kruger National Park supports large prides of lions,leaopards, cheetah and wild dogs.
  2. You could follow nocturnal wildlife on exhilarating spot-lit night drives.
  3. Enjoy the perfect balance of luxurious comforts and unrivalled diversity of life forms with lodges offering world-class tourism facilities.
  4. Walking safaris and bush walks provide a close-up view of the African bush.
  5. A birding destination with regular sightings of several species including the striking lilac-breasted roller and the majestic fish eagle.
